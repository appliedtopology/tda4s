FiniteFieldIsField

org.appliedtopology.tda4s.FiniteFieldIsField
class FiniteFieldIsField(val p: Int) extends Field[Int]

Extension for a finite field with characteristic p. The mathematical structure of a field requires that the characteristic is prime to ensure the existence of multiplicative inverses modulo p, except for the additive identity.

Value parameters

p

The field characteristic, all operations in the field are performed modulo p
